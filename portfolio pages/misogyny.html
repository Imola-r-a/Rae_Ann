Julia Gillard Misogyny Speech

Motion Graphic

This piece of work is a motion graphic where I created the visuals to follow the iconic speech by Julia Guillard in the Australian parlement where she is calling out a man for their misogynistic comments. I loved the speech from the moment I heard it, I love to see a woman standing up for what’s right and that’s exactly what Julia did. It was my first experience with a motion graphic and Adobe After effects. I think it’s a great software I’d like to develop in.
